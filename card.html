Cow

Cows are social animals, and they naturally form large herds. And like people, they will make friends and bond to some herd members, while avoiding others.

  • Scientific Name: Bos taurus
  • Average weight: 720 kilograms
  • Average Lifespan: 15 years
  • Habitat: Grasslands and pastures

Cows are the most common type of large domesticated ungulates. In some regions, such as India, cattle have significant religious meaning. In 2009, cattle became one of the first livestock animals to have a fully mapped genome.
